VIU



Worked as the Content Manager and Designer on an augmented reality app








VIU is an augmented reality app focused on orienting parents, new students, and visitors to IU's campus & history. This app allows users to walk around IU's campus in real time and tap on significant landmarks they see through their phone's camera and learn more about them. In addition, users are able to walk around and examine 3D models with their camera's phone, as well as experience 360 video whilst learning about a certain part of campus.

My Responsibilities:

  • Managed the tone, clarity and story of informational descriptions.

  • Edited, imported, and placed 3D models in engine in Unity.

  • Communicated and maintained a clear vision and scope between developers and executive grant owners Michael Chabin & Jonathon Racek.

  • Developed and delivered a playable demo of the app at the IU Bicentennial.

Informational Descriptions

It was important for the description cards to convey information about the history of IU's campus in a fun and engaging way. I ensured that the history behind it was relevant to showing off IU's most interesting aspects, and editing the diction to be clear, consice, and easy to read.
