As narrative lead on The Shadows That Linger, a point and click murder mystery game, I worked with designers, writers, and artists to craft a story that worked in tandem with mystery solving mechanics in a paranormal-noire atmosphere.

Act Outlines

To maintain communication between designers and writers, I wrote act outlines that were regularly updated as I iterated on the narrative. They included dialogue choice references, story beats, and the purpose behind conversations between characters.

VIU

As content manager on VIU, an augmented reality app aimed at educating new and visiting people on Indiana University's campus history, I worked with alongside the project's software developer to create an engaging experience reading the informational description cards of various interactable 3D models.

Card Descriptions

I managed what stories of IU's campus history would be covered, as well as editing the card descriptions to fit a form of non-technical and universal English that would be easily understood by people of all ages and non-native speakers.
