Monty



Worked as the Designer and Programmer in a in a unit management puzzle platformer








Monty is a game developed by my classmates and I in University in our first game workshop class. Monty is a unit-management puzzle platformer in which the player uses its gummy bears to stretch and bounce to solutions.

During this project I developed the following skills:

  • Learned the process of iteration through redeveloping Monty's concept multiple times in order to reach our vision for the player experience.
  • Rapidly prototyped a gummy bear controller.
  • Developed the skills necessary to maintain a design document and update it regularly according to the state of our game.
