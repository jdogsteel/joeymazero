About




I am a narrative designer, writer, and programmer working on the indie title The Shadows That Linger out of Bloomington, Indiana. I believe that at the intersection between interactivity and storytelling lies the most potent experiences media has to offer. It is my passion to push the boundaries of digital worlds and stories to form more lasting and meaningful relationships with their human counterparts.

I've written and designed everything from systemic branching narratives to item descriptions and lore. If you are looking to deliver an impactful narrative or enrich your game's world, I'd love to get in contact. Feel free to check out the projects I've been working on, or take a look at my resume.
