About

Some things about Joey




Joey is a writer, designer, and programmer. Throughout his four years at Indiana University, he was a part of many teams ranging from video games to virtual/augmented reality. Joey has garnered many milestones in his development as a creative, such as showing Ekter and the Cub at the Indy PopCon his freshman year in 2017. In 2019, he was invited to run a demo of VIU at a convention that IU president McRobby attended as well as tried his team's app. Most recently he attended GDEX as a representative for his team on The Shadows That Linger. He continues run a radio show called The Jam Incident, which started in the summer of 2019 with a close friend at university.


Joey projects to graduate from Indiana University in 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in Informatics, with his concentration being in game design. He also graduated with a minor in poetry, in which he developed important soft skills. When he isn't writing or playing a game, he can be found playing his guitar, petting his dog, or trying his best to get out into nature and hike.
